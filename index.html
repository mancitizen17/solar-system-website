A Cosmic Neighborhood

The solar system is a vast and fascinating place, consisting of our Sun and everything that orbits it. This includes the eight planets, dwarf planets, moons, asteroids, and comets. It's a cosmic neighborhood held together by the Sun's immense gravity.
